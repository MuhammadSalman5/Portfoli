    Project

    FYP-CoalMineControl&Monitoring SystemUsing IoT Basically, our idea is based on the Project named "Coal Mine Control & Monitoring System using IoT"(Design for the Health & Safety of labors) work in the Coal mines. Our Project helps to Monitor and Control the following physical parameters inside the Coal Mine i.e., Temperature, Humidity, Smoke, Light Intensity (Control Through LDR), Oxygen Level and Hazard Gases (For Example). This makes the Coal mine a safety mining and the major disaster preservation are solved through our project.
